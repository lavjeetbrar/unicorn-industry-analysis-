## Dataset Schema

### dates
- company_id: Unique company identifier
- date_joined: Date company became a unicorn
- year_founded: Year company was founded

### funding
- company_id: Unique company identifier
- valuation: Company valuation in USD
- funding: Total funding raised
- select_investors: Key investors

### industries
- company_id: Unique company identifier
- industry: Industry category

### companies
- company_id: Unique company identifier
- company: Company name
- city: Headquarters city
- country: Headquarters country
- continent: Headquarters continent
